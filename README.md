## 💡 Why These Topics?

These three areas form the backbone of most medium and hard DSA problems.

* **Trees** help in understanding hierarchical data structures and recursive thinking.
* **Graphs** introduce traversal, connectivity, shortest paths, and many real-world modeling problems.
* **Dynamic Programming** teaches optimization by breaking problems into overlapping subproblems.

A solid grasp of these topics makes solving complex algorithmic problems significantly easier.

---

## 📈 Learning Approach

For every topic, I follow a structured process:

1. Learn the underlying concept.
2. Understand common patterns and techniques.
3. Solve **Easy → Medium → Hard** problems.
4. Record time and space complexities.
5. Revisit problems that required hints.
6. Document important observations and edge cases.
